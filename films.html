SkillsUSA TV Production

My classmate Jimena Castillo and I submited this project to SkillsUSA for the TV Production competition. I worked as the assitant director and key grip for this project. We drove to Texas A&M University for a college tour event called Aggieland Saturday. We filmed for about 4 hours and then drove back to our school where we recorded the voice over and edited the video in about 4 hours. This project showed us the work and effort that goes into a on-site shoot with a short deadline.
